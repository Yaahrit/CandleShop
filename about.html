Vesper & Vine

Est. 2019

A Narrative of Light and Earth

Rooted in the quiet coastal city of Charleston, Vesper & Vine began as a personal meditation — our founder, Flora Merrit, sought to capture the soft-edged memories of foggy morning walks and the sun-drenched amber glow of her childhood summers.

What started as an experiment in a small kitchen has bloomed into a commitment to slow beauty. We believe that a candle is more than an object; it is a ritual that anchors you to the present moment, transforming the atmosphere of a room the way the golden sun fills a clearing light.

Every scent is a testament to our dedication to the artisan process — no chemicals, no synthetic fillers, just the pure essence of nature captured in glass.

"Scent is the most intimate doorway to our inner sanctuary."
Our Commitment

Purity in Every Element

We meticulously source every ingredient to ensure they are as natural as the earth that brings them to your home.

Natural Wax

100% renewable, American-grown soy wax free of additives, blended with coconut wax for premium scent throw and clean burn with no soot or paraffin.

Essential Oils

Pure botanical extracts and phthalate-free fragrance oils, carefully blended for an even, natural aroma that fills your space without overwhelming it.

Clean Burn

Lead-free cotton and wood wicks crafted for an even pour and long, consistent burn — creating a serene atmosphere every time you light the flame.

Sustainability

Designed for a Second Life

Sustainability is not a feature at Vesper & Vine; it is the foundation. Our packaging is 100% plastic-free, utilizing recycled paper, soy ink, and reusable vessel lids.

Zero Waste Packaging

Every box is biodegradable and designed for easy recycling.

Reusable Vessels

Our heavy-weight glass jars are hand-sized to be reused as botanical vases, desk organizers, or storage containers.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping

We offset 100% of our shipping emissions through verified carbon credit programs.
