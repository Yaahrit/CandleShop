The Craft

Honest Ingredients, Patiently Prepared

Vesper & Vine is anchored in the preservation of slow craftsmanship. Every single candle is hand-poured in our Baltimore, Maryland atelier, utilizing only 100% natural, domestic soy wax, lead-free cotton wicks, and custom-blended botanical fragrance oils. We create clean, slow-burning scent profiles designed to soothe and elevate your space.

Hand Poured in Micro-Batches

Meticulously crafted in batches of nine to guarantee fragrance consistency and burn quality.

100% Natural Soy Wax

Clean, non-toxic, and renewable soy wax sourced sustainably from domestic farms.

Custom Scent Alchemy

Complex olfactory layers composed of pure essential oils and fine perfume compounds.

Our Philosophy

Poured by hand, guided by nature.

Each Vesper & Vine candle is a testament to the slow living movement. We use sustainably sourced soy wax and essential oils, hand-poured in a small Baltimore atelier. Our candle studio is committed to the belief that the quiet beauty of nature's elements can transform every scent into a story of pure elegance and environmental integrity.

I. Patient Sourcing

We partner exclusively with domestic soy farms that prioritize crop rotation and organic harvesting methods.

II. Mindful Formulation

Our fragrance layers are curated patiently, allowing essential compounds to bond for maximum hot throw consistency.

III. Architectural Vessels

Heavyweight glass and natural cotton wicks are chosen to guarantee clean sootless burns and beautiful secondary utilities.
