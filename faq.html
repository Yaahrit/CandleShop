All Vesper & Vine candles are made with 100% natural soy wax, sourced from American-grown soybeans. We blend it with a small amount of coconut wax for optimal scent throw and a smooth, clean burn. Our wax is free from paraffin, additives, and synthetic dyes.

We use a carefully curated blend of pure essential oils and phthalate-free fragrance oils. Every scent is formulated to be non-toxic and safe for your home. Our fragrance blends are never tested on animals and are compliant with IFRA standards.

For the best experience: trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn, allow the wax to melt fully across the surface on the first burn (about 2-3 hours), never burn for more than 4 hours at a time, and keep away from drafts. This ensures an even burn and maximum scent throw throughout the candle's life.

Tunneling (when the wax burns down the center leaving wax on the sides) usually occurs when the candle isn't burned long enough on the first use. Soy wax has a "memory" — the first burn should last until the entire surface has melted. If tunneling has occurred, try wrapping aluminum foil around the top edge to trap heat and help the wax pool evenly.
