The Artisanal Process

Each Vesper & Vine candle is a labor of time and patience. We use a proprietary blend of sustainably harvested soy wax and lead-free cotton wicks. Every jar is hand-finished in our small studio, ensuring that the scent throw is perfectly balanced from the first light to the very end of the burn.

Sustainable

FSC-certified packaging and renewable soy wax sourced from local farms.

Handcrafted

Poured in small batches of twenty to ensure consistent quality and scent concentration.

Recyclable

Our signature amber glass jars are designed to be reused as botanical vases or storage.
